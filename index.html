How Does It Work?

Wondering How It All Works? ...It's easy...

By joining you become an independent travel agent. You will have access to your own system to book all of your own travel or for a client base and access our incredible systems and partners to make these bookings.

Some of the partners you will have access to include Royal Caribbean, Club Med, Direct Ferries, Flexible Autos and Ratehawk plus many more. From staycation breaks and days out tickets to worldwide trips and holidays we can book everything!

There are no targets so you earn from this business what you want to earn, all on your terms. To make sure you get the most out of the systems and programme, there’s some mandatory training that you can do online and in your own time (approx 40 mins.)

We also have twice weekly new starter calls to take you through how best to get started. We are covered by Tour Operator Payment Protection System (TOPPS), Package Holidays and Travel Trade Act and Data Protection Commission so you can be assured your bookings are in safe hands!

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get started?

    Simply complete the ‘get started link’ above and we’ll contact you via WhatsApp to get you set up and get you going with our mandatory training. Within a few hours you could be a qualified Independent Travel Agent.

  • What if I join and change my mind?

    There is no contract meaning that you can leave at any time. If you cancel in the first 30 days you get a 30 day full money back guarantee. After the first month, you can cancel at anytime and payments stop immediately.

  • Do you have to have travel experience?

    No experience is required. We have extensive FREE training with all our suppliers as well as constant in house company training. The training is all online, it’s accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and done on your terms around your current work & family commitments. It’s completely flexible.

  • Are you restricted to the holidays you can book?

    No, not at all! You can use all of our host agency & preferred partners to make bookings or contact any hotel or tourism company worldwide!

  • Do you need a big social media following?

    Absolutely not! Many people build their whole travel business on bookings for friends and family and word of mouth.

  • Do you need to be based in the Republic of Ireland?

    No, we are currently operating in the US, UK and some of the Caribbean and we will soon be expanding into Canada.

  • Is this a network marketing company?

    There are two separate businesses, the travel business and the marketing business. When you join the travel business, you book a holiday and earn travel commissions - It’s that simple!

    There is also the option to join the marketing company and refer your friends too so they can earn from travel too. But this is a completely separate business opportunity that opens up more revenue streams. If you would like to know more info on the marketing business we can chat you through how this business works too.

  • How much does it cost?

    There is a one off payment of €169 to set up your home based travel agency. Then its only €39 a month (which you start paying 30 days after joining).

  • How and when are commissions paid?

    Commissions are paid 30 - 90 days after travel has completed. Once the host company has received the commission, it is split 70/30 (70% to you & they keep 30%) before sending it over to you.
